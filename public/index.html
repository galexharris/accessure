Color Blind

Symptoms: Some colors appear different to him

Frustrations: He feels he misses information that is presented only with color

Design Tips:

  • All color combinations should have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal-size text​
  • Instead of color, use symbols, outlines, bolded text, shapes etc.​

Jane

Visually Impaired

Symptoms: Has some vision, but hard to see details and small text

Frustrations: Websites are very hard to use due to small text and poor contrast. Non-responsive websites don't adjust well to zooming in.

Design Tips:

  • Use large text with high contrast and appropriate line spacing
  • Ensure content is responsive with window zoom
  • All important content is made clear and easy to find
